About Us


Aama's Kitchen brings you the warmth of homemade Nepali food, just like your mother's cooking.It is a palce where you get authentic nepali cuisine made with Aama's love. It was establised in 1997 AD.Since then this restaurant has been runned as a family restaurant with all the members of the family working and other few outside workers. When you enter this place it feels very like at home and calm, peaceful and cozy.

At Aama’s Kitchen, we believe food is more than just a meal — it is an experience that brings people together. From delicious Nepali specialties to modern favorites, every dish is prepared with care to give our customers a memorable dining experience in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.
